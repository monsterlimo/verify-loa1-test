How Verify works

Verify works with certified companies to prove that you are who you say you are. The company will ask you for some details and double check them against data from other sources such as mobile phone providers, credit agencies, DVLA or HM Passports Office.

Using certified companies means that the NHS won't handle your personal data, and you get a choice of who does.

Verifying your identity takes about 5 minutes to complete, and there’s no charge for this service.
